Who are we ?

ELADE/ is a design team that makes cool stuff like websites, promotions, photoshoots, video production and more.
We decided to separate the team in 3 departments, one for designing called ELADE/ design , one for web developement called ELADE/ code and the last one is for video production, animation and motion design, it's called ELADE/ motion.
